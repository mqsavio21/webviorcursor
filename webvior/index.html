What is VIOR?

VIOR is a community academy based for Indonesian Valorant players, particularly those who identify as vstreamers or vtubers. Dedicated to fostering talent, promoting competitive gaming, and creating a community for passionate gamers, We strive to support and develop players on mechanical skills, mental fortitude, and the strategic aspects of Valorant.

As part of our commitment to excellence, VIOR encompasses principles to empower skills and elevate talents by creating our own academy for nurturing new talent, and an affiliate program to expand our reach in the community.
